Origin Story

We opened our flagship location in Chula Vista to share our family recipes with all of our neighbors. Soon our neighbors came to enjoy our family recipes with their friends and families and our H-Thai-ML family started to grow. Now 10 years later we have 4 kitchens across the county and we can't wait to feed you.
